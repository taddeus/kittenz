The workshop is a tab that opens up fairly early in the game. It contains two important functions:

  • Upgrades: the ability to purchase upgrades that increase productivity and/or functionality
  • Crafting: The ability to craft certain resources

Note: Below, the Description is in red when it does not match the actual cost or effect.

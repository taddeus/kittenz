Solar Revolution(Edit)

Cost Faith 750
Gold 500
Description Accumulated faith will give a small boost to resource production
Effects Percentage bonus given is (sqrt(1 + faith / 125)-1)/2.
Accumulated faith needed for a certain bonus is (pow((2 * bonus + 1),2) -1) * 125
This yields unlimited diminishing returns at lower values: 1% at 1000 faith, 5% at 15000 faith, and so on.
This is capped at 1000% with limited diminishing returns.
It is further increased by 10% per Transcendence Level (and by Black Obelisks.)
This gives bonuses for the total pool size, not the amount currently saved up.
Prerequisite Total Faith 1000

Apocrypha(Edit)

Cost Faith 5000
Gold 5000
Description Grants the ability to discard accumulated faith to improve effectiveness of praying
Prerequisite Faith 100K

The faith bonus persists through resets. , but yields diminishing returns:

You get 1 apocrypha point per 1M faith in your praise pool at reset. This amount is multiplied by (1+transcendence tier)2 before being being added to your hidden apocrypha pool. The game then uses the formula (((square root(1 + 80 * apocrypha points) -1) /2) * 10) to turn this into your bonus%.

Transcendence(Edit)

Cost Faith 7500
Gold 7500
Description Unlocks additional religion upgrades
Effects Allows you to purchase some religious abilities multiple times.
(Solar Revolution, Apocrypha, and Transcendence can be bought only once)
Price increase is 2.5x per upgrade.

Also allows you to sacrifice your apocrypha bonus and turn it into Transcendence tiers which unlocks Cryptotheology insights.
Prerequisite Total Faith 125K
